Digital garden

Last updated on November 1, 2020

Digital gardening is a way of storing and growing your knowledge. In the form of a growing and regularly updated graph of nodes.

Nodes

Nodes are small light-weight pieces of text, that is usually written about a single concept, fact or idea.

Edges

Edges or links between nodes make navigation easy in the gardens. Raom-like bidiractional links makes them even more useful.

Refs: Ness Labs: How to set up your own digital garden
